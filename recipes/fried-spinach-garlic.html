Fried Spinach and Garlic

Description

Freshly wok fried spinach and garlic. The perfect dish to enjoy after a swim in the ocean.

Makes a great side dish to a freshly fried fish. Enjoy with a bowl of rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 300g of baby spinach
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • a pinch of sea salt
  • a pinch of sugar

Steps

  1. Chop up the garlic into small pieces.
  2. Pre-heat the wok to a high temperature.
  3. Add the oil to the wok.
  4. Add the garlic to the wok, closely followed by the spinach.
  5. Add a pinch of salt and sugar to the wok.
  6. Mix the spinach and galrlic continuously until the leaves have all shrivelled.
  7. Take the wok off the heat and serve with a bowl of rice or noodles.
