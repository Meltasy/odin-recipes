Fried Eggs and Tomatoes

Description

Freshly wok fried eggs and tomatoes with a sprinkling of pepper. The perfect dish to enjoy after surfing in the ocean.

Add spring onion or spicy peppers for extra flavour. Enjoy with a bowl of rice or a freshly baked baguette.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • a pinch of ground pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • a handful of spring onions or spicy peppers

Steps

  1. Chop up the tomatoes into bite sized chunks.
  2. Beat or whisk the eggs until the egg whites and yolks are well mixed.
  3. Add a sprinkling of ground pepper to the egg mix.
  4. Finely slice the spring onions or spicy peppers.
  5. Pre-heat the wok to a high temperature.
  6. Add the oil to the wok.
  7. Add the egg mix to the wok and stir to keep from sticking to the sides.
  8. Once the egg is no longer liquid, add the tomatoes to the wok and keep stiring.
  9. Take the wok off the heat and sprinkle the spring onions or spicy peppers over the egg and tomatoes.
  10. Serve with a bowl of rice or a freshly baked baguette.
